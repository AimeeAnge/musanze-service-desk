\# Musanze District Digital Service Desk

\## 1. Real Users

**Potato Aggregators:** Field agents and warehouse managers collecting harvests from farmers and distributing to markets.

**Event Organizers (INES):** University staff and student coordinators managing academic workshops, seminars, and campus events.

**Home Stay Owners:** Small-scale hospitality providers in the Musanze/Kinigi area managing local guest stays.

\## 2. Problem Being Solved

**Accuracy:** Eliminates manual calculation errors (order values) that lead to financial loss for aggregators.

**Efficiency:** Replaces slow paper-based logs with instant digital registration for high-volume participant tracking at INES.

**Transparency:** Provides guests and buyers with immediate, clear cost estimates and provides owners with a digital audit trail.

\## 3.Fields used by all services

*All services within this desk utilize a standardized data schema for consistency:*

**Client Name:** (e.g., Farmer name, Event attendee, or Guest name).

**Service/Item:** (e.g., Potato Grade A, Workshop Registration, or Night Stay).

**Quantity:** Numerical unit (e.g., Kgs, Seats, or Days).   **Unit Price:** The fixed cost per single unit in RWF.

**Total Value:** Automated calculation `Quantity` × `Unit Price`).

\## 4. Expected Screen Outputs

**Live Total Preview:** A real-time, dynamic display that updates the "Total Value" field as the user types the quantity or price, ensuring clarity before submission.

**Success Message:** A clear visual confirmation (e.g., "Order Confirmed" or "Registration Successful") upon clicking Submit, including a unique Transaction ID.

**Records Table:** A dashboard view below the input form displaying a searchable history of all entries, allowing operators to track participant lists or sales volumes at a glance.
